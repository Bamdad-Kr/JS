7 ta toop darim cheshmi andaze o vazneshoon yekie ama yekishon saboktare mikhaim ba estefade az tarazoo sabokaro bekeshim biroon

  1. 3 ta topo mizarim ye tarafe tarazo
  2. 3 ta dige ham ye taraf
  3. ye done ham biroon
  4. age tarazoo barabar bashe on bironie sabokast
  5. dar qeire in soorat ye tarafe saboktare
  6. hala on tarafe saboktar ke 3 tast dobare 1 kisho mizarim biroon onyekiaro ro tarazoo test mikonim
  7. to in marhale dige kamelan moshakhas mishe ke sabok kodoome
